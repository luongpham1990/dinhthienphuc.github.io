Kraken Mobile

Introduction

Made to be the perfect companion for wherever life takes you, the Razer Kraken Mobile gives you impressive audio quality in a form factor that’s incredibly mobile. This durable yet lightweight headset is made for Apple™ iOS with an in-line mic and media controls, so you have easy access to all features even when you’re on the move.

Beneath the striking neon, the Razer Kraken Mobile is packed with powerful, high-quality drivers enclosed in comfortable, extra-large circumaural ear cushions, allowing you to enjoy an immersive audio experience while turning heads everywhere.

Rugged Construction Meets Maximum Comfort

Constructed to provide maximum durability without compromising on comfort, the Razer Kraken Mobile is lightweight yet able to withstand the daily abuse of life on the go. Regardless of whether you’re tuning out your daily commute or getting in the zone while you hit the track, you’ll be comfortable for hours on end.
